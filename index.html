About

Builder.
Not a one-trick dev.

Five years of full-stack work has taught me one thing: a well-designed API and a clean frontend that respects it is a better combination than any framework fashion. I work across PHP/Laravel and Node.js on the server, and vanilla JavaScript on the client — picking the tool that fits the problem, not the other way around.

I've worked with startups, small businesses, and agencies across Nigeria and remotely. The best projects I've done started with a clear problem — not a feature list. I also build and sell commercial templates on Envato, which means I've shipped real products to real buyers at scale.

I'm based in Enugu, Nigeria — a city with a growing tech community and fibre that mostly behaves. Both are improving faster than people expect.
